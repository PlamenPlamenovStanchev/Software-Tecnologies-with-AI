Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer trial memberships?

Yes! We offer a free 7-day trial pass so you can experience our facilities and classes before committing to a membership.

Can I freeze my membership?

Absolutely. We understand life gets busy. You can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year without penalties.

What's your cancellation policy?

Month-to-month memberships require 30 days notice. Annual memberships have a flexible cancellation option within the first 30 days.

Are personal trainers available?

Yes! Our certified trainers offer individual sessions and group training packages. Consultations are free and available by appointment.

What's included in Premium membership?

Premium includes 24/7 gym access, unlimited group classes, 4 PT sessions per month, nutrition coaching, and member discounts.

Do you have childcare services?

Yes, we offer supervised childcare during business hours for members. Age requirements and booking information available upon request.
